Member Registration

NP Membership is for non profit organizations and there is no fee:

  • Access to networking events, such as business mixers, industry-specific meetups, and social gatherings.
  • Opportunities to connect with other business owners, entrepreneurs, and professionals in the community.
  • Access to online member directories for networking and collaboration.

Bronze Membership:

  • Listing in the Chamber's online business directory, allowing potential customers to find and connect with member businesses.
  • Opportunities for business spotlights and features on the Chamber's website, social media, or newsletters.
  • Access to marketing resources, such as promotional materials, advertising discounts, and sponsorship opportunities for Chamber events.

Silver Membership:

  • Representation on local, regional, or national issues affecting businesses, with the Chamber acting as an advocate for members' interests.
  • Access to government officials, policymakers, and community leaders through Chamber-led initiatives and events.
  • Involvement in shaping local economic development and business-friendly policies.

Gold Membership:

  • Workshops, seminars, and webinars on various topics pertinent to business growth, such as marketing, finance, leadership, and technology.
  • Training programs and resources to enhance skills and knowledge, including business coaching, mentorship programs, and industry-specific certifications.
  • Access to industry reports, market research, and business intelligence to stay informed about relevant trends and opportunities.
