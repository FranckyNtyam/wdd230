Name

Yaounde Chamber of Commerce

Purpose

Yaounde chamber of commerce main purpose is to work with voluntary NGO or people to help the members of our community to become self-reliance. The website will provide various educational resources, seminars and other learning opportunities.

Scenarios

  1. Where to get information that can help to become self reliance?
  2. What are upcoming seminars for this month?
  3. Where can I sign up to become a member of the chamber?
  4. Who are the officers and board of directors of this community?
  5. Who can I contact when I need help?
